Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to establish an internet presence for the company in order to help grow and retain the customer base to ultimately increase sales revenue. To accomplish this, the site will create an awareness of the exceitement of whitewater rafting and present the companies trip offerings in a compelling manner and encourage site visitors to act.

Audience

Who are the target customers? The target custumer are typically middle class and above people that are generally physically active (and perhaps various organizations representing such persons). Person would be aged from youth age to middle age.

What do they want? They are seeking for adventurous and exciting recreation far outside the everyday experience which they can afterwrds talk to others about.

What do they except to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? They except to receive further understanding of what the whitewater rafting experience is about, what the company's offerings are, and to find out how to particpate including the price.
