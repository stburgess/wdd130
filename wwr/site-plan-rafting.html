Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to establish an internet presence to promote the company in order to help grow and retain the customer base and ultimately increase sales revenue.

Audience

Who are the target customers? The target customers are typically from a middle class and above, aged from earlz teens to middle aged and generally physically active. Organizations representing this demographic that sponsor extra-curricular activies are also a target.

What do they want? They are seeking exciting alternative recreation that satisfies their desire for adventure in the outdoors.

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? They expect to receive further understanding of what the whitewater rafting is, what the company's offerings are, and the price to particpate.
